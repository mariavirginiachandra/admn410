Durham House of Pets accepts:

Durham House of Pets does not accept:
  • COD (Cash on Delivery)
  • Gift Cards from other merchants
  • (EBT) Electronic Benefits Transfer
  • International Credit & Debit Cards
Mixed Payment Methods:
  • You can use up to 3 gift cards and 1 credit/debit card or cash at one time (4 total)
  • Gift Cards cannot be used to purchase other Gift Cards
  • Only one credit/debit card can be used at one time
  • PayPal and Cash cannot be mixed with any payment methods
